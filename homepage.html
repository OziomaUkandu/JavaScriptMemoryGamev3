Welcome

We at Fantastic memory games strive to make enjoyable yet educational games for your kids.


The aim is for kids to build their short term memory and build on retaining information at a decent rate.


We have different levels of difficulty to choose from which are easy, medium and hard.

Three Categories

    • Animals
    • European Flags
    • Fruits



How to play the game:

  1. So depends which section of the game you are playing (animals, flags or fruits)
  2. Choose two facing down cards to turn them over;
  3. If it is a match, the cards will stay turned over showing the image;
  4. Otherwise they will be turned over again;
  5. The game ends when you match all the pairs;

Recommendations.

We Recommend that you're child plays our game for atleast 5 to 10 minutes daily.
The idea is that they'll eventually progress through the levels of each game as their memory improves.
This kind of matching game can be very entertaining for kids. This game is good exercise for the brain.
It also helps to improve your concentration.
