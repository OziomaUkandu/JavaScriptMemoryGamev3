Welcome...

We at fantastic memory games strive to make enjoyable yet educational games for your kids.

The Aim is for the kids to build their short term memory and build on retaining information at a decent rate.

We have different levels of difficulty to choose from which are easy, medium or hard.This game is aimed at kids 10 and below..

How To Play The Game..

  1. So depends which section of the game you are playing (Fruits, Animals, European Flags)

  2. Choose two facing down cards to turn them over

  3. If it is a match, the cards will stay turned over showing the image

  4. Otherwise they would be turned back over again

Recommendations

We recommend that you're child plays our game for about 5-20 minutes daily.

The idea is that they'll eventually progress through the levels of each game as their memory improves.

This kind of matching game can be very entertaining for kids.

This game is good exercise for the brain.

It also helps to improve your concentration.
